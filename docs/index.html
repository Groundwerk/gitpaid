Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Gitpaid is open-source (MIT). Since it runs on Cloudflare Workers, small teams usually fall well within Cloudflare's generous free tier for compute and storage.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. You host the application. Data never leaves your infrastructure and you fully control who has access to your employee or financial information.

What about T4s?

Gitpaid tracks all YTD data necessary to generate employee T4s and CRA T4 uploads. We provide export tools to make year-end filing with the CRA straightforward and audit-proof.

Can I customize it?

It's fully open source. Fork the repo, tweak the UI, or add your own custom logic as your business needs evolve. It's built with modern React and Tailwind.

What regions do you support?

Gitpaid currently supports Ontario with the rest of Canada coming soon. Non-Canadian regions will be supported in future versions.

Do you handle payments?

No. We will look into integrating different payment modules in the future to help facilitate this.
